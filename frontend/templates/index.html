Is this video good for your kid?

MindSafe analyzes a YouTube video's transcript and scores it on six developmental dimensions — pacing, language, story, social-emotional learning, fantasy, and interactivity — to flag content that may be overstimulating for young children.

How it works

A three-tier analysis pipeline that always returns a result — and never sends the video off your device.

1

Captions

If the video has captions, MindSafe pulls the transcript straight from your browser session and analyzes it instantly.

2

In-browser Whisper

No captions? A Whisper speech-to-text model runs locally in your browser (WebGPU / WASM) to transcribe the audio — no server, no upload.

3

Scoring

The transcript is scored across six dimensions, age-normalized, and passed through LLM safety guardrails before a recommendation is shown.
